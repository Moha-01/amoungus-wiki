Skins



Skins are a type of cosmetic that characters can wear. They usually cover the lower half of the body. Due to the exclusion of the shop on the Nintendo Switch edition of Among Us, skins from the MIRA HQ, Polus, and The Airship skin bundles are unobtainable on the system.
