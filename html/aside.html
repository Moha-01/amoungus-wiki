Latest Map

The Airship, often shortened as Airship, is the fourth map for Among Us, based on the Toppat Clan's Airship in the Henry Stickmin game Infiltrating the Airship. The Airship is the largest map in Among Us, includes more tasks, and has seventeen new locations.
